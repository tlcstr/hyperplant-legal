Hyper Plant — Privacy Policy

Last update: 2025-08-16

Summary: the app is fully offline. Your data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to any servers.

  • Data: plants, care plans, logs, settings — local (SQLite/MMKV).
  • Permissions: notifications for reminders; can be disabled in system/app settings.
  • Sharing: none. No analytics, no tracking.
  • Storage: all data stays on device; uninstalling the app removes it.
  • Contact: support@hyperplant.app
